# Introduction: The Builder Reality Check

Before you build your first product, you need to unlearn everything you think you know about building.

You can code. You've shipped features for other people's products. You've followed tutorials, built side projects, maybe even freelanced. But when it comes to building your own product — something that solves a real problem for real users — something stops you.

This introduction is a reality check. It's here to confront the uncomfortable truth most developer guides skip: why you haven't built anything yet, and what's actually stopping you.
